Pro Features

Watch your impact grow. Your Tree Counter tracks the cumulative contribution your subscription makes toward reforestation efforts, one morning at a time.

Make Sprout yours. Choose from a curated collection of serene, nature-inspired themes that set the tone for a calm and focused start to your day.

Our Mission

Mornings That Matter

50%

Half of all subscription revenue generated by Sprout Alarm Clock is allocated toward tree-planting initiatives. This is embedded in how we run this business — not an afterthought, not a campaign.

We believe a small daily habit — waking up — can connect to something much larger. Every active Sprout Pro subscriber is part of a growing community whose collective presence helps fund reforestation efforts around the globe.

Revenue allocation is managed by Sprout's operating budget. Subscribers are not making a direct charitable donation. Tree-planting contributions may vary based on operational costs.
